What is Casteism?

Casteism is a social problem in which each religion is sub-divided into different castes and these castes again into sub-castes. The culture of each caste is different even though they all belong to one religion. Among these castes, certain are given a high status and others a low status, depending upon their caste occupation.

Because of Castes, different people are treated differently.
For example, people of a higher Caste will be given more privileges than people of lower Castes, irrespective of their values.
